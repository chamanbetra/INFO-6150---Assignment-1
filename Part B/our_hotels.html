Boston, Massachusetts
Boston is the capital and largest city of the state of Massachusetts in the United States. It is one of the oldest cities in the country, with a rich history dating back to the early days of American colonization. The city is known for its many colleges and universities, including Harvard, MIT, and Boston University, as well as its cultural and historical landmarks, such as the Freedom Trail, the Boston Tea Party Museum, and the USS Constitution Museum. Boston is also a major center for medicine and biotechnology, with many leading hospitals and research institutions located in the area. The city is situated on the east coast of Massachusetts, and is home to a diverse population of around 694,583 people.
